Ingredients
===========
* Yeast
    - 1 1/2 t Yeast (might want to experiment with decreasing in the future)
    - 4 t Granulated Sugar
    - 1 1/2 C Warm Water (100-110 F)
* Dough
    - 3 1/2 C AP Flour
    - 2 t Salt
    - 1 T Brown Sugar
    - 2 t Olive Oil (after initial knead)
    - Eggs for egg wash
* Boil Bath
    - 2 Q Water
    - 1/4 C Honey / Syrup / Sugar



Instructions
============

1. Combine Yeast, Sugar, and Water and let sit for 5 mins
2. Mix Flour and Salt in a large bowl
3. Create a divot and pout in the yeast mixture
4. Mix well, potentially adding 1/4 C water if needed
    4.1 Optionally let dough rest for a few minutes here
5. Knead dough by hand for 10-15 minutes
6. Incorporate Oil into dough and knead
7. Place in oiled bowl, cover and let rise for 1-2 hours (* Maybe lengthen this time?)
8. Punch the dough down to release the air, can also do some squishing when it's out of the bowl
9. Divide dough into 8 equal pieces, or into 100g portions
10. Flatten out the pieces, lay out on floured parchment paper
11. Let rise for 10-15 mins
12. Prepare water bath solution and bring to simmer
13. Preheat over to 425 as water heats
14. Shape bagels by finger poke or cut method and dip in water bath to boil ~1 min per side
15. Place on parchment paper lined sheet, brush with egg wash afterwards
16. Bake for 20-25 mins @ 425 or until golden brown.  Might want to rotate sheet with 8-10 mins left
